What we build

Month 1 setup, refreshed monthly

Prospect targeting (two tiers)

Tier 1

20-30 deeply researched target companies per month with key contacts, recent activity, and potential pain points. Personalized connection messages for each.

Tier 2

40-50 prospects identified from LinkedIn search using predefined criteria (title, industry, company size). Niche-specific connection message templates.

Total pipeline: 60-80 targeted prospects per month, split across active niches. Prospect lists refreshed monthly so the pipeline never runs dry.

LinkedIn outreach playbook

  • Connection request messages tailored by prospect type (multiple variants to keep it natural)
  • Post-connection DM sequence that builds rapport over weeks before suggesting a virtual coffee
  • Liking and commenting on prospects' posts to build visibility and familiarity before and during the outreach sequence
  • Comment bank categorized by post type so Linda always has the right response ready
  • All written in Grant's voice, referencing ESP's real client results where relevant

What gets executed each week

  • 15-25 targeted connection requests from Grant's LinkedIn
  • Like and comment on prospect posts to build visibility and rapport
  • Follow up with new connections via the DM sequence
  • Follow-up DMs to connections from previous weeks
  • Flag warm responses for Grant
  • All activity logged in the tracking spreadsheet

Monthly: New batch of researched prospects loaded into the system (Tier 1 and Tier 2). 30-minute review with Colin: what's working, which niche is converting, adjust targeting. Refresh outreach templates based on response data.

What Grant does

  • Shows up for warm conversations when they're flagged
  • 30-minute monthly review with Colin to see the numbers and approve any changes

Nothing else. The hunting is handled.
